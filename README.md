**Supply Chain Volatility**

Volatility in raw materials is a critical risk factor for pasta producers. Wheat price fluctuations, driven by weather risks, geopolitical tensions, and logistic complexities, can significantly impact cost structures. Recent commodities forecasts highlight scenarios where key inputs like flour could face upward pressure due to tighter seasonal crops and elevated global risk factors.

Additionally, trade tensions and tariffs are emerging as systemic supply risks. Example developments in global food sector equities illustrate inflationary pressures affecting producers in volatile currency environments, prompting investor reassessments of exposure in emerging markets.
